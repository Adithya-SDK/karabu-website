Our Story

How It Began

Born from a Quiet Idea

It started with a conversation over bad coffee — and a shared frustration that a great café shouldn't be hard to find. Two friends, one bold idea, and a small rented space on Nawala Road.

The early days were humble. Long hours, hand-painted menus, a second-hand espresso machine. But every detail was chosen with care — the bean, the brew, the light above the counter, the chair by the window.

Today, KARABU is a place the community calls their own. A second living room. A favourite corner of the city — and we wouldn't have it any other way.

Est. 2019
